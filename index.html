Rudra

Hi, I am Rudra. I am 23 years old and I am a singer-songwriter based in Agartala. I have started my journey as an independent musician from 2020 and since then I have released four singles.There is an EP on the way soon. Also, during this time I got featured on some online magazines and playlists and I have put together some of them in the features section.In this website, you will get the first notifications regarding my upcoming works and feel free to check out the links. I have attached all the links to my social platforms and for more I have also attatched a linktree list so that you don't wander away. Upcoming

I have been working upon my debut EP for quite some time. The name of the EP is called "Keh de". The album comprises of 4 songs and the songs are about issuing the freedom to the heart so that it's speech can be heard.Maybe sometimes the heart acts like a child, maybe sometimes it's the bridge between the self and the divine but inspite of all the extremities, there is a beauty to this wonderful mess and each of it's stories. The album will be out on 22nd July 2022 on all the platforms.
